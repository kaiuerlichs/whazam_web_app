About

We provide you with albums, song and lyrics to the artist of your choice. A completely original idea never done before *cough* *cough*

About the team.

We are a bunch of Web Development students from the University of Dundee and we programmed this brilliant masterpiece of web-app. Thankfully, our lecturer Mike taught us very well and got us through this “unique” first semester at uni.

About the website.

This web app is built using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, allowing us to create a unique and detailed user experience by making use of the Bootstrap CSS framework and the vue.js library. By taking data from the “TheAudioDB” and “lyrics.ovh” APIs, we show our users exactly what they want to know about their favourite artists and music.
